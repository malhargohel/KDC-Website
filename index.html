BAPS Kids Diwali Celebrations: The Beacon of Light

The Kids Diwali Celebration is proudly hosted by BAPS Swaminarayan Sanstha, offering a unique blend of fun and profound values. KDC is a vibrant celebration where children are gently guided to learn the true meaning of Diwali through engaging creativity, captivating stories, and the spirit of seva (selfless service).

Inspired by Pramukh Swami Maharaj’s words - “In the joy of others lies our own” - the event helps kids grow spiritually, spread kindness, and become lights in their communities.
